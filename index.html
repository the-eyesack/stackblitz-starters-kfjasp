Drivin' for McLaren

Oscar Piastri races for the McLaren Formula 1 team after a controversial (and bada**) move from Alpine. He has consistently delivered strong performances, earning podium finishes and even securing his first Grand Prix victory. Piastri's future at McLaren looks promising, and many fans are excited to see what he can achieve in the years to come.

Since Oscar Piastri joined the McLaren team in 2023, the team has shown significant improvement in their performance.

  • Podium Finishes: Both Piastri and his teammate, Lando Norris, have consistently secured podium finishes throughout the season, demonstrating the team's competitiveness.
  • Constructor Standings: McLaren has climbed the ranks in the Constructors' Championship, securing a higher position compared to previous seasons. Currently, McLaren is 1st in the Constructor's Championship.
